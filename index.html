Yes, you can select the specific fund or cause you want to support during the donation process.

Are donations tax-deductible?

Yes, donations to registered organizations are often eligible for tax deductions. Please check the details in your country.

Your donation is allocated directly to the fund or cause you select, ensuring maximum impact.
